ABOUT US

Peep into the nature of funny and the lives of funny apes with Humour Sapiens.

  • We truly, deeply, madly believe that it takes a lot to go on stage and stand in front of the intimidating or ready-to-take-offence audience and make them laugh, with or without joke.
  • Undeniably, this art form has a huge potential to keep people hooked. Humour Sapiens wants to make a small contribution to give this challenging art form its much deserved credit.
  • Our intent is to keep you up to date on news, views, reviews and interviews of the big and soon-to-be-big of the Indian stand-up comedy circle. We will also keep you posted on the upcoming comedy events which you must watch and where, and the ones you can pass!
  • We hope to delight you. See you around!
